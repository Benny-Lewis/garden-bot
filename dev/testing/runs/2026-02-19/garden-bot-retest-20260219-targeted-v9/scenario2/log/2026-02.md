# 2026-02
- 2026-02-18: Transplanted tomato seedlings to raised bed.
- 2026-02-21: Diagnosed early blight (Alternaria spp.) on tomato seedlings. Symptoms: yellow lower leaves with concentric brown spots, progressing upward. Contributing factors: overhead evening watering, cool wet weather, early outdoor transplant. Treatment: prune affected leaves, switch to base watering, mulch, copper fungicide every 7-10 days, row cover at night, light fertilizer.
