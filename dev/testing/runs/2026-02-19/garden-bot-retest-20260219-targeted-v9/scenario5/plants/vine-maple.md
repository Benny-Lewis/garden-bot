# Vine Maple
- Botanical: Acer circinatum
- Type: Small multi-stem deciduous tree
- Zone: 5–9
- Light: Partial shade to full shade
- Size: 10–20 ft tall, 10–15 ft spread
- Area: Side yard (west-center alcove, focal canopy over bench)
- Notes: PNW native. Spring red new growth, fall orange. Naturally multi-stemmed — fits narrow spaces. No pruning needed in shade conditions.
