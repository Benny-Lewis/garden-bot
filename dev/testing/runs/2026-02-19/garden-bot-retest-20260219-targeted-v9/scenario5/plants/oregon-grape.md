# Oregon Grape
- Botanical: Mahonia aquifolium
- Type: Evergreen shrub
- Zone: 5–9
- Light: Partial shade to full shade
- Size: 5–8 ft tall, 4–5 ft spread
- Area: Side yard (east entry screen)
- Notes: PNW native. Holly-like evergreen foliage, yellow spring flowers, blue berries. Used as a screening hedge at entry to create hide-and-reveal effect. Low maintenance — no regular pruning required.
