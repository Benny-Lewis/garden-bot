# Wild Ginger
- Botanical: Asarum caudatum
- Type: Evergreen groundcover
- Zone: 4–8
- Light: Partial shade to full shade
- Size: 4–6 in tall, spreading
- Area: Side yard (west exit, groundcover layer)
- Notes: PNW native. Heart-shaped glossy leaves form a dense mat. Slowly spreading — excellent low groundcover under shrubs. No mowing, no pruning. Aromatic when crushed.
