# Salal
- Botanical: Gaultheria shallon
- Type: Evergreen shrub
- Zone: 5–9
- Light: Partial shade to full shade
- Size: 3–5 ft tall, spreading
- Area: Side yard (west exit planting)
- Notes: PNW native. Leathery evergreen leaves, pink-white spring flowers, dark berries. Excellent for shady screening. Spreads gradually — contains well along fence line.
