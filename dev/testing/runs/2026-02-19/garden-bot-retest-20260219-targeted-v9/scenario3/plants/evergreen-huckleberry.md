# Evergreen Huckleberry
- Botanical: Vaccinium ovatum
- Type: Evergreen shrub (native)
- Zone: 8b
- Location: Front yard — foundation hedge (both sides of walkway)
- Quantity: ~8 (hedge spacing 2 ft on center)
- Planted: Planned spring 2026
- Role: Foundation hedge, stealth edible (berries Aug–Sep)
- Care: Clip 2–3× per year to maintain 2 ft height. Acidic soil preferred. Low water once established.
