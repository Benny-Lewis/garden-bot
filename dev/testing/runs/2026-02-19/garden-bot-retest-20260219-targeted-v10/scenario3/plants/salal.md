# Salal

- Botanical: Gaultheria shallon
- Type: Evergreen native shrub
- Zone: 6–9
- Size: 2–5 ft tall, spreading
- Area: Front yard — right accent bed
- Role: PNW native, stealth edible berries
- Sun: Full sun to deep shade
- Water: Low once established
- Notes: Iconic PNW understory shrub. Dark purple edible berries in late summer (sweet, used by indigenous peoples). Glossy evergreen foliage popular in floral arrangements. Extremely tough and adaptable.
