# Oregano

- Botanical: Origanum vulgare
- Type: Herbaceous perennial herb
- Zone: 4–9
- Size: 12–18 in tall, spreading
- Area: Front yard — foundation strip (right side)
- Role: Herb edge, stealth edible
- Sun: Full sun
- Water: Low
- Notes: Thrives in hot, dry foundation strip. Purple-pink flower clusters attract pollinators mid-summer. Cut back in early spring for fresh growth.
