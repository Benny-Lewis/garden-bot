# Heuchera

- Botanical: Heuchera micrantha (native) or hybrid
- Type: Evergreen native perennial
- Zone: 4–9
- Size: 12–18 in tall and wide
- Area: Front yard — right accent bed
- Role: Native ornamental, foliage color accent
- Sun: Full sun to part shade
- Water: Low to moderate
- Notes: PNW native coral bells. Colorful foliage (purple, coral, lime varieties available) provides contrast against green shrubs. Delicate flower spikes attract hummingbirds. Evergreen in Zone 8b.
