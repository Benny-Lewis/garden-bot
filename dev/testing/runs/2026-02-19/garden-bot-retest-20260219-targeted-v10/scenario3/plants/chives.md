# Chives

- Botanical: Allium schoenoprasum
- Type: Herbaceous perennial herb
- Zone: 3–9
- Size: 10–15 in tall, clumping
- Area: Front yard — foundation strip (right side)
- Role: Herb edge, stealth edible
- Sun: Full sun
- Water: Low to moderate
- Notes: Purple globe flowers in late spring are highly ornamental and edible. Mild onion flavor. Clumping habit stays tidy. Divide every 2–3 years.
