# Dwarf Fig 'Petite Negra'

- Botanical: Ficus carica 'Petite Negra'
- Type: Deciduous fruit tree (dwarf)
- Zone: 7–10
- Size: 4–6 ft tall, 3–4 ft wide
- Area: Front yard — left main bed (anchor)
- Role: Architectural form, stealth edible
- Sun: Full sun
- Water: Low once established
- Notes: Dark purple-black figs in late summer. Dramatic lobed foliage. May need fleece wrap in coldest Zone 8b winters (below 15°F).
