# Thyme

- Botanical: Thymus vulgaris / T. serpyllum (creeping)
- Type: Evergreen herb/groundcover
- Zone: 4–9
- Size: 2–6 in tall, spreading
- Area: Front yard — foundation strip (left side)
- Role: Herb edge, stealth edible
- Sun: Full sun
- Water: Low
- Notes: Creeping thyme for foundation strip where reflected heat promotes vigorous growth. Fragrant when stepped near. Tiny purple flowers attract pollinators. Mix culinary and creeping varieties.
