# Alpine Strawberry

- Botanical: Fragaria vesca
- Type: Evergreen perennial groundcover
- Zone: 3–9
- Size: 6–8 in tall, spreading
- Area: Front yard — left main bed groundcover (with kinnikinnick)
- Role: Edible groundcover
- Sun: Full sun to part shade
- Water: Low to moderate
- Notes: Tiny intensely-flavored berries from June through frost. Non-running (clumping) habit, much tidier than garden strawberries. Interplanted with kinnikinnick for mixed groundcover texture.
