# Rosemary

- Botanical: Salvia rosmarinus
- Type: Evergreen herb/shrub
- Zone: 7–10
- Size: 3–4 ft tall and wide
- Area: Front yard — left main bed, foundation strip (creeping form)
- Role: Evergreen structure, stealth herb
- Sun: Full sun
- Water: Low once established
- Notes: Upright form in main bed, creeping form ('Prostratus') in foundation strip. Fragrant, pollinator-friendly blue flowers in spring. Thrives in reflected heat near foundation.
