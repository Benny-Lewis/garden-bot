# Blueberry

- Botanical: Vaccinium corymbosum (highbush)
- Type: Deciduous fruit shrub
- Zone: 4–8
- Size: 4–6 ft tall, 3–4 ft wide
- Area: Front yard — left main bed (trio)
- Role: Fall color mass, stealth edible
- Sun: Full sun
- Water: Regular, acidic soil preferred
- Notes: Trio planting for cross-pollination and visual mass. Outstanding red/orange fall color. Suggest varieties: 'Duke' (early), 'Bluecrop' (mid), 'Elliott' (late) for extended harvest June–September.
