# Red Huckleberry (Vaccinium parvifolium)

- Area: Side yard — north bed, east half
- Role: Deciduous screening shrub with edible berries
- Light: Partial shade (natural habitat)
- Water: Low once established
- Size: 4-8 ft tall, 3-4 ft spread
- Qty: 3-4 (space 3 ft apart)
- Notes: Deciduous — less winter screening than ferns; red berries June-Aug
