# Red Flowering Currant (Ribes sanguineum)

- **Type:** Native deciduous shrub
- **Location:** Front yard, left bed
- **Size:** 6–10' tall × 6' wide
- **Sun:** Full sun to part shade
- **Edible:** Berries edible (tart, best in jams)
- **Interest:** Pendulous pink-red flower clusters in early spring; hummingbird magnet
- **Role:** Mid-layer spring color, native pollinator support
- **Care:** Prune after flowering to shape; drought tolerant once established
