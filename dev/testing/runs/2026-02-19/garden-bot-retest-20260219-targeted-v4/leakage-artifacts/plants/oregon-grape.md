# Oregon Grape (Mahonia aquifolium)

- **Type:** Native evergreen shrub
- **Location:** Front yard, foundation strip ×2 (transition points)
- **Size:** 3–5' tall × 3' wide
- **Sun:** Full sun to part shade
- **Edible:** Tart blue berries (best in jelly/syrup) — late summer
- **Interest:** Holly-like evergreen leaves, yellow flower clusters in spring, blue berries, bronze-purple winter foliage
- **Role:** Native structural accent at foundation transitions; evergreen year-round
- **Care:** Very low maintenance; can sucker — remove unwanted shoots; Oregon state flower
