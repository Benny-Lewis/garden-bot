# Western Serviceberry (Amelanchier alnifolia)

- **Type:** Native deciduous tree/large shrub
- **Location:** Front yard, planting strip (street tree)
- **Size:** 12–15' tall × 10' wide
- **Sun:** Full sun
- **Edible:** Sweet purple berries (June) — excellent fresh, in pies, or dried
- **Interest:** White flower clusters in early spring, edible berries, orange-red fall color
- **Role:** Street-level focal tree, seasonal interest from sidewalk, native + edible
- **Care:** Minimal; prune to single or multi-stem form; drought tolerant once established
