# Rosemary (Salvia rosmarinus)

- **Type:** Evergreen shrub/herb
- **Location:** Front yard, foundation hedge ×8 (4 per side of door)
- **Size:** 2–3' tall × 2' wide each, sheared to hedge form
- **Sun:** Full sun
- **Edible:** Leaves — year-round culinary herb
- **Interest:** Evergreen needle-like foliage, blue flowers in late winter/spring
- **Role:** Continuous foundation hedge, frames house, edible year-round
- **Variety:** 'Tuscan Blue' or 'Arp' for upright hedge form and cold hardiness
- **Care:** Excellent drainage required; do not overwater; shear to shape 2× per year; hardy to ~10°F ('Arp') or ~15°F ('Tuscan Blue')
