# Calendar

## February
- Profile created. Planning phase.

## March
- Soil amendment window opens — good time to add compost to clay beds.
- Start cool-season seeds indoors (lettuce, kale, peas).

## April–May
- Transplant cool-season starts outdoors after last frost (~mid-April in Portland).
- Direct-sow pollinator wildflower seeds in front yard once soil warms.

## June–September
- Dry season — establish watering routine for new plantings.
- Harvest window for summer edibles.

## October–November
- Fall planting season for native shrubs and perennials (rain does the watering).
- Mulch beds before winter.
