# Oregano
- Botanical: Origanum vulgare
- Type: Herbaceous perennial
- Area: Front yard — right herb bed
- Size: 1-2 ft tall, 1-2 ft wide
- Sun: Full sun
- Water: Low — drought-tolerant
- Edible: Yes — leaves for cooking
- Notes: Spreading habit fills in nicely between sage and lavender. Pink-purple flowers in summer attract pollinators. Cut back in early spring for fresh growth.
