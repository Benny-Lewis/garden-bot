# Alpine Strawberry
- Botanical: Fragaria vesca
- Type: Evergreen perennial groundcover
- Area: Front yard — left drift groundcover layer
- Size: 6-8 in tall, spreading
- Sun: Full sun to part shade
- Water: Moderate
- Edible: Yes — small fragrant berries spring through fall
- Notes: Non-running clumping habit. Neat evergreen groundcover that produces tiny intensely flavored fruit. Pairs with kinnikinnick for a mixed groundcover drift.
