# Sage
- Botanical: Salvia officinalis
- Type: Evergreen sub-shrub/herb
- Area: Front yard — right herb bed
- Size: 2 ft tall, 2 ft wide
- Sun: Full sun
- Water: Low — drought-tolerant
- Edible: Yes — leaves year-round
- Notes: Silvery-green foliage provides textural contrast. Purple flower spikes in early summer. Excellent drainage. Replace every 3-4 years as plants become woody.
