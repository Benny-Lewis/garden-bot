# Lavender
- Botanical: Lavandula angustifolia
- Type: Evergreen sub-shrub
- Area: Front yard — right herb bed
- Size: 2 ft tall, 2-3 ft wide
- Sun: Full sun
- Water: Low — drought-tolerant
- Edible: Yes — flowers for culinary use
- Notes: Fragrant silver foliage with purple summer bloom. Major pollinator magnet. Needs excellent drainage. 'Hidcote' or 'Munstead' are compact varieties suited to Portland.
