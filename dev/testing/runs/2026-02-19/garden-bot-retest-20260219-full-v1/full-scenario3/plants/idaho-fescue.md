# Idaho Fescue
- Botanical: Festuca idahoensis
- Type: Perennial bunchgrass
- Area: Front yard — left drift accent
- Size: 1-2 ft tall, 1 ft wide
- Sun: Full sun
- Water: Low — drought-tolerant
- Edible: No
- Native: PNW native
- Notes: Fine blue-green foliage adds airy texture among blueberries and groundcover. Clumping habit stays tidy. 'Siskiyou Blue' cultivar has especially good color.
