# Evergreen Huckleberry
- Botanical: Vaccinium ovatum
- Type: Evergreen shrub
- Area: Front yard — foundation bed
- Size: 4-6 ft tall, 3-4 ft wide
- Sun: Part shade to full sun
- Water: Low once established
- Edible: Yes — small dark berries in fall
- Native: PNW native
- Notes: Dense glossy foliage resembles boxwood — ideal stealth edible. Slow-growing, low maintenance. Tolerates the slightly shadier north-facing foundation position. New growth is bronze-red.
