# Rosemary
- Botanical: Salvia rosmarinus
- Type: Evergreen shrub/herb
- Area: Front yard — foundation bed
- Size: 3-4 ft tall, 2-3 ft wide
- Sun: Full sun
- Water: Low — drought-tolerant
- Edible: Yes — leaves year-round
- Notes: Architectural upright form. Blue flowers in late winter/spring attract pollinators. Excellent drainage required. Hardy in Zone 8b Portland.
