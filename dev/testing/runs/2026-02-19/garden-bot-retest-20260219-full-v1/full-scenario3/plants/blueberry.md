# Blueberry
- Botanical: Vaccinium corymbosum
- Type: Deciduous shrub
- Area: Front yard — left drift, 2 plants
- Size: 4-6 ft tall, 3-4 ft wide
- Sun: Full sun
- Water: Regular, consistent moisture
- Edible: Yes — berries July-August
- Notes: Brilliant red-orange fall color. Needs acidic soil (pH 4.5-5.5). Plant two varieties for cross-pollination (e.g., 'Duke' + 'Bluecrop'). Mulch with bark or pine needles.
