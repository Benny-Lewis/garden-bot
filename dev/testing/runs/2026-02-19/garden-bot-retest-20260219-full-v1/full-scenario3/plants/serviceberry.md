# Serviceberry
- Botanical: Amelanchier alnifolia
- Type: Small multi-stem tree/large shrub
- Area: Front yard — left drift anchor
- Size: 12-15 ft tall, 10-12 ft wide
- Sun: Full sun to part shade
- Water: Low once established
- Edible: Yes — berries in June-July
- Native: PNW native
- Notes: Four-season interest. Spring white bloom, summer fruit, orange-red fall color, structural winter branching. Attracts birds.
