# Creeping Thyme
- Botanical: Thymus serpyllum
- Type: Evergreen groundcover/herb
- Area: Front yard — walkway edge, left drift edge
- Size: 2-4 in tall, 12-18 in spread
- Sun: Full sun
- Water: Low — drought-tolerant
- Edible: Yes — leaves for cooking
- Notes: Aromatic when stepped on near walkway edge. Purple flowers in summer attract pollinators. Softens hard edges of walkway and beds. Tolerates light foot traffic.
