# Red Huckleberry

- Botanical: Vaccinium parvifolium
- Type: Deciduous shrub
- Zone: 6–9
- Area: Side Yard (north bed, west and east ends)
- Qty: 2
- Light: Partial shade
- Water: Low once established
- Size: 4–8 ft tall
- Notes: Light, airy branching at corridor entry/exit points. Edible red berries in summer. Bright green stems provide winter interest.
