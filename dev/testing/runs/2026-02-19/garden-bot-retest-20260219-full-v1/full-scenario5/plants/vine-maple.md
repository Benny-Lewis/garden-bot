# Vine Maple

- Botanical: Acer circinatum
- Type: Deciduous small tree / large shrub
- Zone: 5–9
- Area: Side Yard (north bed, center)
- Qty: 1
- Light: Partial shade to full shade
- Water: Low once established
- Size: 10–20 ft tall, multi-stemmed
- Notes: Canopy anchor for the corridor. Graceful branching structure provides Japanese-garden character year-round. Fall color is orange-red.
