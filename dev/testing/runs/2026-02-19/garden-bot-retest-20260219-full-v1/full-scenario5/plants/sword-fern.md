# Sword Fern

- Botanical: Polystichum munitum
- Type: Evergreen fern
- Zone: 5–9
- Area: Side Yard (north bed, two clusters)
- Qty: 2 clusters (3–5 fronds each)
- Light: Partial shade to full shade
- Water: Low once established
- Size: 2–4 ft tall, 3 ft spread
- Notes: Evergreen backbone of the north bed. Fills gaps between Vine Maple and Red Huckleberry. Zero maintenance — remove old fronds in late winter if desired.
