# Salal

- Botanical: Gaultheria shallon
- Type: Evergreen shrub
- Zone: 6–9
- Area: Side Yard (south bed, center drift)
- Qty: 1 drift (3–5 plants)
- Light: Partial shade to full shade
- Water: Low once established
- Size: 2–3 ft tall, spreading
- Notes: Dense evergreen groundcover drift. Leathery leaves give year-round structure. Spreads slowly via rhizomes to fill in naturally.
