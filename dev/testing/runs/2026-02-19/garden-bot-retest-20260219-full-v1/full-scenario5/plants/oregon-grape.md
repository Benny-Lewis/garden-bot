# Oregon Grape

- Botanical: Mahonia nervosa (low Oregon grape)
- Type: Evergreen shrub
- Zone: 5–9
- Area: Side Yard (south bed, two positions)
- Qty: 2 clusters (3–4 plants each)
- Light: Partial shade to full shade
- Water: Low once established
- Size: 1–2 ft tall, spreading
- Notes: Low-growing native with holly-like leaves. Yellow spring flowers, blue berries in fall. Echoes the role of nandina in Japanese gardens but is fully native. Evergreen structure year-round.
