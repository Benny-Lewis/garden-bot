# Inside-out Flower

- Botanical: Vancouveria hexandra
- Type: Deciduous groundcover perennial
- Zone: 5–8
- Area: Side Yard (south bed, west and east ends)
- Qty: 2 patches (5–7 plants each)
- Light: Partial shade to full shade
- Water: Low once established
- Size: 8–12 in tall, spreading
- Notes: Delicate spring blooms with reflexed petals (hence "inside-out"). Native woodland ephemeral that fills in gently. Deciduous — pairs with evergreen Salal and Oregon Grape for seasonal contrast.
