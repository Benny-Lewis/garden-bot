# Rhubarb

- Species: Rheum rhabarbarum
- Variety: 'Victoria' or 'Crimson Red'
- Type: Edible perennial
- Location: Front yard, east bed (mid-bed)
- Role: Edible perennial, bold foliage
- Size: 2-3 ft tall, 3 ft wide
- Sun: Full sun to part shade
- Soil: Tolerates clay well; thrives in moist conditions
- Harvest: April-June (stalks only â€” leaves are toxic)
- Care notes: Don't harvest first year. Remove flower stalks to keep energy in roots. Divide every 4-5 years. Mulch in winter.
