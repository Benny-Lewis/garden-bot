# Calendar

## February
- Clean and prep tools
- Plan front yard bed layout and mark edges

## March
- Amend soil in bed areas with compost (work into top 6-8 inches)
- Build mounded acidic bed for blueberries (east bed, near house)
- Plant blueberry bushes (3) in mounded bed
- Plant rhubarb crown (east bed)
- Plant artichoke starts (west bed) â€” or start from seed indoors early March

## April
- Plant dwarf fig ('Little Miss Figgy') in west bed
- Plant lavender on slight mound for drainage (west bed)
- Plant sage (east bed)
- Plant echinacea (east bed)
- Plant ornamental grass (east bed)
- Edge curved beds and define walkway borders

## May
- Plant creeping thyme along walkway borders (both beds)
- Plant low herbs (thyme, oregano) in west bed border
- Mulch all beds (2-3 inches)

## June-August
- Harvest blueberries (staggered by variety)
- Harvest artichoke buds (May-June)
- Harvest rhubarb stalks (April-June, skip first year if newly planted)
- Water deeply during dry spells, especially fig and blueberries

## August-October
- Harvest figs

## November
- Mulch blueberries with pine bark/needles
- Mulch fig base heavily for winter protection (first 1-2 years)
- Cut back echinacea and ornamental grass after frost
- Mulch artichoke crown for winter survival
