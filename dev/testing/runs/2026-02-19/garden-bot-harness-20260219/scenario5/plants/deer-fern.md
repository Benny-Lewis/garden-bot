# Deer Fern (*Blechnum spicant*)

- Role: Accent fern
- Location: Side yard — north entry
- Native range: PNW coastal forests
- Size: 1-2' tall
- Sun: Shade to part shade
- Water: Moderate — prefers consistent moisture
- Soil: Humus-rich, acidic
- Evergreen: Yes
- Notes: Finer texture than sword fern, provides contrast. Distinctive fertile fronds stand upright above the sterile rosette. Plant in groups of 3.
