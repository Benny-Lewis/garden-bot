# Oceanspray (*Holodiscus discolor*)

- Role: Fence screening
- Location: Side yard — north entry, along east fence
- Native range: PNW, BC to California
- Size: 8-12' tall, 6-8' wide
- Sun: Full sun to part shade
- Water: Very low once established — drought tolerant
- Soil: Adaptable, tolerates poor soil
- Bloom: White cascading plumes, June-July
- Wildlife: Host plant for many native butterfly and moth species
- Notes: Will grow well above the 4' chain link fence within 2-3 seasons. Cascading bloom habit echoes Japanese wisteria aesthetic. Prune after flowering to control size.
