- Location: backyard south raised bed

## Disease: Early Blight (Alternaria solani)
- Diagnosed: 2026-02-21
- Symptoms: yellow lower leaves with dark brown concentric (target) spots, progressing upward
- Conditions: cool wet weather, overhead evening watering, no mulch
- Sources: NC State Extension, UMN Extension, Wisconsin Extension, UMD Extension
