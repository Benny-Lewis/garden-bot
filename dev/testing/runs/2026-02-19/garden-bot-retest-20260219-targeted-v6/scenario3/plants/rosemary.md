# Rosemary

- Species: *Salvia rosmarinus*
- Type: Evergreen shrub (herb)
- Location: Front yard, foundation strip
- Qty: 3
- Zone: 8b
- Sun: Full sun
- Mature size: 3–4 ft tall, 2–3 ft wide
- Bloom: Blue-purple, late winter–spring
- Edible: Leaves — culinary herb
- Notes: Upright variety recommended ('Tuscan Blue' or 'Arp'). Thrives in Portland with good drainage. Reads as an ornamental evergreen shrub — classic stealth edible. Blooms attract early pollinators.
