# Alpine Strawberry

- Species: *Fragaria vesca*
- Type: Evergreen perennial groundcover
- Location: Front yard, walkway borders (both sides)
- Zone: 8b
- Sun: Full sun to part shade
- Mature size: 6–8 in tall, spreading
- Bloom: White, spring–fall
- Fruit: Small intensely flavored berries, spring through fall
- Notes: Non-running type — forms tidy clumps, ideal for edging. Fruits continuously. Looks like a decorative border plant, not a food crop. 'Alexandria' or 'Yellow Wonder' are good varieties.
