# Lavender

- Species: *Lavandula angustifolia*
- Type: Evergreen sub-shrub
- Location: Front yard, right accent bed
- Qty: 2
- Zone: 8b
- Sun: Full sun
- Mature size: 2–3 ft tall and wide
- Bloom: Purple, June–August
- Edible: Flowers — culinary (baking, teas, infusions)
- Notes: English lavender handles Portland's winter wet better than French types. 'Hidcote' or 'Munstead' are compact, well-suited varieties. Excellent pollinator plant.
