# Western Serviceberry

- Species: *Amelanchier alnifolia*
- Type: Deciduous small tree/large shrub
- Location: Front yard, left bed (focal tree)
- Qty: 1
- Zone: 8b
- Sun: Full sun
- Mature size: 15–20 ft tall, 10–15 ft wide
- Bloom: White clusters, early spring
- Fruit: Edible blue-purple berries, June–July
- Fall color: Orange to red
- Native: Yes (PNW)
- Notes: Multi-stem form recommended for modern look. Berries taste like mild blueberry, good fresh or in baking. Birds also love them — expect to share.
