# Sword Fern
- Botanical: Polystichum munitum
- Type: Evergreen fern
- Zone: 5–9
- Light: Partial shade to full shade
- Size: 2–4 ft tall, 3–4 ft spread
- Area: Side yard (east entry, base planting)
- Notes: PNW native. Extremely low maintenance — no watering once established, no pruning. Provides year-round green texture under Oregon grape screen.
