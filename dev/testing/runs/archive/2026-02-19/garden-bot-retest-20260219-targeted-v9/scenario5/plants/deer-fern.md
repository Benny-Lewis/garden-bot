# Deer Fern
- Botanical: Blechnum spicant
- Type: Evergreen fern
- Zone: 5–8
- Light: Partial shade to full shade
- Size: 1–2 ft tall, 1–2 ft spread
- Area: Side yard (west exit planting, paired with salal)
- Notes: PNW native. Compact, tidy habit. Distinctive fertile fronds stand upright above spreading sterile fronds. No maintenance once established.
