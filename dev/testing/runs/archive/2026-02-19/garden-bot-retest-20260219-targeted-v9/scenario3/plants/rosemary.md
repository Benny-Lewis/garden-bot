# Rosemary (Standard Form)
- Botanical: Salvia rosmarinus
- Type: Evergreen shrub, trained as standard (tree-form)
- Zone: 8b
- Location: Front yard — right feature bed
- Quantity: 2
- Planted: Planned spring 2026
- Role: Year-round structural evergreen, culinary herb, fragrance
- Care: Full sun, excellent drainage. Trim to maintain lollipop form 2× per year. Protect from hard freezes below 15°F.
