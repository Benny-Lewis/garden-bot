# Blueberry 'Pink Lemonade'
- Botanical: Vaccinium 'Pink Lemonade'
- Type: Deciduous shrub (rabbiteye hybrid)
- Zone: 8b
- Location: Front yard — left main bed
- Quantity: 1
- Planted: Planned spring 2026
- Role: Mid-season fruit (July), pink berries for visual interest, fall foliage
- Care: Acidic soil (pH 4.5–5.5). Slightly more heat tolerant than northern highbush. Prune oldest canes late winter.
