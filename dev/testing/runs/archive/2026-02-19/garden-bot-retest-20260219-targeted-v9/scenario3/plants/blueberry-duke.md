# Blueberry 'Duke'
- Botanical: Vaccinium corymbosum 'Duke'
- Type: Deciduous shrub
- Zone: 8b
- Location: Front yard — left main bed
- Quantity: 1
- Planted: Planned spring 2026
- Role: Early-season fruit (June), fall foliage color
- Care: Acidic soil (pH 4.5–5.5). Mulch with gravel. Prune oldest canes in late winter. Water deeply in summer.
