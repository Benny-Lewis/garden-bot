All follow-ups are recorded. The key takeaway: **the biggest single change you can make is stopping overhead evening watering** — that alone dramatically reduces the leaf wetness hours that fuel this disease.

Your plants should recover fine once you remove the affected leaves and improve conditions. Early blight rarely kills tomato plants outright, especially when caught early like this.
