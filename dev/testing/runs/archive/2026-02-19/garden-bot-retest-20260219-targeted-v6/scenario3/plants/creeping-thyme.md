# Creeping Thyme

- Species: *Thymus serpyllum*
- Type: Evergreen groundcover
- Location: Front yard, right open area (thyme lawn)
- Coverage: ~72 sq ft
- Zone: 8b
- Sun: Full sun
- Mature height: 2–4 in
- Bloom: Pink-purple, summer
- Edible: Leaves — culinary herb
- Notes: Walkable, releases fragrance when stepped on. Needs well-drained soil. Plant plugs 6–8 in apart for full coverage in one season. No mowing required. Tolerates light foot traffic.
