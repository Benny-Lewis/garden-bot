# Purple Sage

- Species: *Salvia officinalis* 'Purpurascens'
- Type: Evergreen sub-shrub (herb)
- Location: Front yard, right accent bed
- Qty: 2
- Zone: 8b
- Sun: Full sun
- Mature size: 18–24 in tall, 24 in wide
- Bloom: Purple, summer
- Edible: Leaves — culinary herb
- Notes: Purple-grey foliage provides year-round color contrast. More ornamental than common sage with the same culinary use. Drought-tolerant once established.
