# Evergreen Huckleberry

- Species: *Vaccinium ovatum*
- Type: Evergreen shrub
- Location: Front yard, planting strip hedge
- Qty: 5
- Zone: 8b
- Sun: Full sun to part shade
- Mature size: 4–8 ft tall (can be hedged shorter)
- Bloom: Pink-white bell-shaped, spring
- Fruit: Small dark berries, late summer–fall (edible, sweet)
- Native: Yes (PNW)
- Spacing: 3–4 ft for hedge
- Notes: Excellent hedging plant — dense, evergreen, and produces tasty berries. Slow-growing first 2 years then fills in well. Florists prize the glossy foliage.
