# Blueberries

- Species: *Vaccinium corymbosum*
- Type: Deciduous shrub
- Location: Front yard, left bed (around serviceberry)
- Qty: 4
- Zone: 8b
- Sun: Full sun
- Mature size: 4–6 ft tall
- Bloom: White bell-shaped, spring
- Fruit: Edible berries, July–August
- Fall color: Brilliant red
- Soil: Acidic (pH 4.5–5.5) — Portland soils often naturally acidic enough
- Notes: Plant at least 2 varieties for cross-pollination. Good Portland varieties: 'Duke' (early), 'Bluecrop' (mid), 'Elliott' (late). Beautiful fall foliage doubles as ornamental.
