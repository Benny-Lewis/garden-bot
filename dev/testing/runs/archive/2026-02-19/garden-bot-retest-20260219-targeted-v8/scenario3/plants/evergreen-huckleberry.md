# Evergreen Huckleberry
- Botanical: Vaccinium ovatum
- Count: 4
- Area: Front yard, right bed back row
- Role: Formal hedge (stealth edible, PNW native)
- Spacing: 3 ft on center
- Harvest: September–October
- Notes: Native evergreen. Tolerates pruning to formal hedge shape. Slow-growing — buy largest available size (3–5 gal). Partial shade tolerant but fruits best in full sun.
