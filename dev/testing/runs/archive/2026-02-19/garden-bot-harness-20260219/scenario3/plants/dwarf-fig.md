# Dwarf Fig ('Little Miss Figgy')

- Species: Ficus carica
- Variety: Little Miss Figgy
- Type: Deciduous fruit tree
- Location: Front yard, west bed (near house)
- Role: Focal point
- Size: 4-6 ft tall and wide
- Sun: Full sun (south-facing exposure)
- Soil: Tolerates clay well
- Harvest: August-October
- Zone hardiness: 7-11 (fine for 8b)
- Care notes: Minimal pruning needed. Protect from hard freezes in first 1-2 years with mulch at base. Water deeply during summer dry spells.
