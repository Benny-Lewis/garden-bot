# Blueberry (3-bush hedge)

- Species: Vaccinium corymbosum
- Variety: TBD â€” recommend a mix of early/mid/late varieties for extended harvest (e.g., 'Duke', 'Bluecrop', 'Elliott')
- Type: Fruit shrub
- Location: Front yard, east bed (mounded, near house)
- Role: Edible hedge, seasonal interest
- Size: 4-6 ft tall each
- Sun: Full sun
- Soil: Mounded acidic mix (peat/sulfur-amended) over native clay
- Harvest: June-August (staggered with variety mix)
- Seasonal interest: White spring flowers, summer fruit, red-orange fall foliage
- Care notes: Mulch with pine bark or needles to maintain acidity. Keep soil consistently moist but not waterlogged. Net if birds are a problem. Prune oldest canes in late winter.
