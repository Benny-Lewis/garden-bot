# Artichoke

- Species: Cynara cardunculus var. scolymus
- Variety: 'Green Globe' or 'Imperial Star' (annual/perennial in 8b)
- Type: Edible perennial
- Location: Front yard, west bed (mid-bed)
- Role: Architectural ornamental + edible buds
- Size: 3-4 ft tall, 4 ft wide
- Sun: Full sun
- Soil: Tolerates clay if not waterlogged; benefits from compost amendment
- Harvest: Late spring-early summer (May-June)
- Care notes: Mulch heavily in winter for perennial survival in 8b. Cut spent flower stalks. May need replanting every 3-4 years as plants decline.
