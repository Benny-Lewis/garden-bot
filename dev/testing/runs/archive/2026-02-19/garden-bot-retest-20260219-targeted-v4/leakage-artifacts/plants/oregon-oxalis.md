# Oregon Oxalis (Oxalis oregana)

- **Type:** Native evergreen groundcover
- **Location:** Side yard, groundcover carpet
- **Size:** 4–8" tall, spreading by rhizomes
- **Sun:** Part shade to deep shade
- **Interest:** Clover-like trifoliate leaves, small white-pink flowers in spring; leaves fold closed in bright sun
- **Role:** Groundplane carpet — fills the planting bed floor with a clean, self-maintaining layer
- **Care:** Very low maintenance; self-spreading in moist shade. Thin edges if it encroaches on path.
