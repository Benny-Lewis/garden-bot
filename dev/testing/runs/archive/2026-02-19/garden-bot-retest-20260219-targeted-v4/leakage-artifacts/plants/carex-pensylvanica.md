# Pennsylvania Sedge (Carex pensylvanica)

- **Type:** Native evergreen sedge groundcover
- **Location:** Front yard, planting strip (mass planting)
- **Size:** 6–8" tall, spreading by rhizomes
- **Sun:** Full sun to part shade
- **Interest:** Fine-textured grass-like foliage, no-mow lawn alternative
- **Role:** Planting strip groundcover — eliminates mowing, low water, clean look
- **Care:** Mow once in late winter to refresh; drought tolerant once established; no fertilizer needed
