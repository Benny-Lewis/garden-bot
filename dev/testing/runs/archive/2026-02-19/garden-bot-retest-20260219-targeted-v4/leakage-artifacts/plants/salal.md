# Salal (Gaultheria shallon)

- **Type:** Native evergreen shrub
- **Location:** Front yard, left bed ×2; Side yard, along fence ×3
- **Size:** 3–5' tall, spreading
- **Sun:** Full sun to deep shade
- **Edible:** Dark purple berries (mild, sweet) — July–September
- **Interest:** Leathery evergreen leaves, pink urn-shaped flowers in spring
- **Role:** Evergreen mid-layer structure, year-round screening
- **Care:** Very low maintenance; can shear to control spread
