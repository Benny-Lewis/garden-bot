# Sword Fern (Polystichum munitum)

- **Type:** Native evergreen fern
- **Location:** Front yard, left bed ×3; Side yard, understory ×3
- **Size:** 3–4' tall fronds
- **Sun:** Full sun to deep shade
- **Interest:** Bold textural evergreen fronds; iconic PNW understory
- **Role:** Groundlayer texture, fills understory beneath vine maple
- **Care:** Remove old fronds in late winter before new growth; virtually indestructible
