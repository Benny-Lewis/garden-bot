# Deer Fern (Blechnum spicant)

- **Type:** Native evergreen fern
- **Location:** Side yard, near vine maple
- **Size:** 1–2' tall, tidy rosette form
- **Sun:** Part shade to deep shade
- **Interest:** Two frond types — spreading sterile fronds and upright fertile fronds; finer texture than sword fern
- **Role:** Textural contrast near the vine maple — more refined and compact than sword fern
- **Care:** Very low maintenance; prefers consistent moisture and acidic soil
