# Blueberries (Vaccinium corymbosum)

- **Type:** Deciduous fruiting shrub
- **Location:** Front yard, right bed ×4
- **Size:** 4–6' tall × 3–4' wide each
- **Sun:** Full sun
- **Edible:** Berries July–August
- **Interest:** White spring flowers, blue-green summer foliage, brilliant crimson fall color, red winter stems
- **Role:** Ornamental mass planting that produces fruit; understory layer beneath fig
- **Care:** Acidic soil (pH 4.5–5.5) — amend with sulfur and peat; mulch with bark; prune oldest canes in late winter; net if birds are heavy
- **Variety suggestions:** Mix early/mid/late for extended harvest — e.g., 'Duke' (early), 'Bluecrop' (mid), 'Elliott' (late), 'Chandler' (large fruit)
