# Red Huckleberry (Vaccinium parvifolium)

- **Type:** Native deciduous shrub
- **Location:** Side yard, east end accent
- **Size:** 4–6' tall, upright open form
- **Sun:** Part shade to full shade
- **Edible:** Small bright-red berries (tart, excellent for jam/baking) — July–August
- **Interest:** Delicate branching, bright green leaves, translucent red berries; often grows on nurse logs in the wild
- **Role:** Edible accent shrub; continues stealth-edibles philosophy from front yard
- **Care:** Minimal; prefers acidic, humus-rich soil. No pruning needed — open form is the appeal.
